# Codex Rules (Operating Charter)
- You may modify existing .ts/.tsx/.css files.
- You may create new files under src/components/** and src/content/**.
- You may run npm scripts (lint/typecheck/build) without asking.
- You do NOT have permission to change package.json dependencies without asking.
- You do NOT have permission to delete routes under src/app/** without asking.
- You do NOT have permission to modify deployment or DNS without asking.
